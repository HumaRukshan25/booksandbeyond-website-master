SUDEEP NAGARKAR



Sudeep Nagarakar (born 26 February 1988) is an Indian novelist and writer of fiction.
His first novel, Few Things Left Unsaid, was published in 2011.
Till 2015, six novels authored by him have been published.
He is the recipient of the 2013 Youth Achievers' Award for writing
and for being one of the highest selling writers in India in romance genre.
His book It started with a friend request was the most popular fiction book of 2013, according to Amazon India.
