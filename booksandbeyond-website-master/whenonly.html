WHEN ONLY LOVE REMAINS

DURJOY DATTA

I've imagined this in my head so many times.
I've always thought about what I would say;
What I would do and how it would all turn out to be.
And every time I would remove some detail...

She's a flight attendant—young, bright and living her dream.
He's a heartbroken singer on his way to becoming big.
She's an ardent fan of his.
He can't imagine why and yet seems to find comfort in her words.
It's the first time they are together and in their hearts both are wishing,
hoping and praying that the night would never end.
That the time they are spending together lasts and lasts...

In the world of imperfection, there is always someone just right for you.
