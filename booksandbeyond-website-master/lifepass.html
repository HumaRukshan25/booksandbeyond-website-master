YOU'RE THE PASSWORD TO MY LIFE

SUDEEP NAGARKAR

We all have that one person in our lives
in whose absence our existence seems meaningless!

Virat and Kavya are like chalk and cheese.
While Virat is cautious and reserved, Kavya is outgoing and likes to lead a life full of reckless fun.
In spite of their differences, they are thick friends and not even Mahek,
the love of Virat's life, can come in the way of that.
But, as happens in every relationship, their friendship is put to the test
when an unforeseen incident hits them all of a sudden.
Can Aditya, along with his cousin, come to their rescue yet again?

You're the Password to My Life is a true story that shows you
how friendship is the only 'ship' that does not sink.
